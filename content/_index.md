This is a list of Ukrainian individuals and NGOs supporting and fundraising for Ukraine. **Do your own research before donating.** I don't have time to thoroughly vet every entry, and inclusion here is not a personal guarantee.

In the words of [@Oksii33](https://x.com/Oksii33): always check for proper reports from previous fundraisers before donating anywhere. Volunteers must be responsible, and it is better to donate to those willing to spend the time delivering trusted, honest reports.
