---
title: "Ukrainian Creators"
---

Ukrainians who make and sell things — jewelry, art, patches, crafts. Buying their work puts money directly into Ukrainian hands and keeps skills and businesses alive through the war.

**How listings get here.** Every entry either links to a proper storefront (Etsy or similar, with buyer protection and reviews) or carries a personal vouch from someone who has actually bought from them. Entries without a storefront are flagged, because DM orders paid by PayPal have no buyer protection.

**Why the caution:** the "Ukrainian artisan, orders via DM" format is heavily copied by impostor networks running identical sad-story scripts with stolen photos. Genuine artisans get drowned out by fakes using the same words. The same [verification checks](/#verify) apply here as for fundraisers.
