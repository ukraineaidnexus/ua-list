# Ukraine Aid Nexus

**[ukraineaidnexus.org](https://ukraineaidnexus.org)** is a curated list of Ukrainian
NGOs, volunteers and creators supporting Ukraine's defence and its people, alongside
a guide to verifying fundraisers, a list of accounts worth following, and a register
of documented scammers.

**Inclusion is not an endorsement.** Do your own research before donating. The site's
[verification guide](https://ukraineaidnexus.org/#verify) explains how.

## Submitting to the list

All submissions go through this repository's issue forms:
**[Open a new issue](../../issues/new/choose)** and pick the right form.

| Form | Use it for |
|---|---|
| **Submit a fundraiser** | A live fundraiser from an org or volunteer already on the list |
| **Suggest an organisation** | A new Ukrainian or foreign org, or an individual volunteer |
| **Suggest a Ukrainian creator** | Someone who makes and sells their work (storefront required) |
| **Report a scammer** | Fraudulent fundraising or fabricated content, with public evidence |
| **Report a correction** | Dead links, outdated info, or an entry you believe is wrong |

## What gets accepted

- **Evidence first.** Fundraiser submissions need a track record of published
  completion reports. Org suggestions need registration links (Opendatabot for
  Ukrainian NGOs, a national charity register for foreign ones) or vouches from
  established volunteers.
- **Scammer reports need public, linkable documentation.** Accusations without
  evidence are not listed.
- **Everything is reviewed by a human before it appears.** Nothing publishes
  automatically.

## Corrections

If an entry about you or your organisation is wrong, use the correction form.
It will be reviewed and fixed or removed if the evidence supports it.
